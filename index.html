Leonardo Mennilli, was born in Mar del Plata, Argentina. When he arrived to the United States of America, he fell in love with the great country. When Covid-19 arrived in 2020, Leo was stuck at home and he started to paint. He continues to learn and create his abstract paintings in acrylic.
